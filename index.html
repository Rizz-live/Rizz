To get this bonus, the host must:
  • Complete at least 4 active days
  • Stream a total of 10 hours in the week
1.3 Rules
  • Withdrawal Rate: 20,000 Ruby = 1 USD
  • Day Change: Daily at 12:00 PM (EST – New York Time)
  • Weekly Period: Sunday 12:00 PM to Sunday 12:00 PM (EST – New York Time)
  • Valid Day: Minimum 120 minutes (2 hours) Live
  • Valid Hour: 60+ minutes = 1 valid hour
  • Minimum Live Time: Sessions below 30 minutes not counted
  • Bonus Payment: Every Monday (in Rubies)
  • Withdrawal: Once per day, Minimum = $10 USD
Agency Change Rules

A host can change agency if:

  • No live for 120 days
  • Cannot contact agency for 2 weeks
  • Both host and agency agree
⚠️ Strict Rule Any misuse or cheating during transfer → Warning / Suspension / Ban

COIN SELLER POLICY

◆
Purpose
›

This policy outlines the eligibility requirements, operational standards, and performance expectations for agencies applying to become Official Coin Sellers on the RIZZ LIVE platform.

01
Eligibility Criteria
›
Minimum Requirement
📌 Qualification ThresholdAgencies must generate 50,000,000 Coins (50M) monthly revenue to qualify as an Official Coin Seller.
Security Deposit Option
  • Agencies below the 50M target may apply with a $1,000 security deposit
  • Deposit is fully refunded once the monthly target is achieved
Maintenance Requirement
  • Approved sellers must sustain 50M monthly revenue
  • Failure for two consecutive months may result in seller status revocation
