To get this bonus, the host must:

1.3 Rules
  • Withdrawal Rate: 20,000 Ruby = 1 USD
  • Day Change: Daily at 10:30 PM (IST)

  • Valid Hour: 60+ minutes = 1 valid hour
  • Minimum Live Time: Sessions below 30 minutes not counted
  • Bonus Payment: Every Monday (in Rubies)
  • Withdrawal: Once per day, Minimum = $10 USD
Agency Change Rules

A host can change agency if:

  • No live for 120 days
  • Cannot contact agency for 2 weeks
  • Both host and agency agree
⚠️ Strict Rule Any misuse or cheating during transfer → Warning / Suspension / Ban

🪙 Coin Seller Guidelines

Coin Seller Policy

📋
Purpose
›

This policy outlines the eligibility requirements, operational standards, and performance expectations for agencies applying to become Official Coin Sellers on the RIZZ LIVE platform.

1
Eligibility Criteria
›
Minimum Requirement
📌 Requirement Agencies must generate 50,000,000 Coins (50M) monthly revenue to qualify as an Official Coin Seller.
Security Deposit
  • Agencies that have not reached the 50M target may apply by submitting a $1,000 security deposit
  • Deposit will be refunded once the target is achieved
Maintenance Requirement
  • Approved sellers must maintain 50M monthly revenue
  • Failure to meet target for two consecutive months may result in seller status cancellation
2
Coin Seller Buying Price
›

Official Coin Sellers must top-up using USDT to receive the corresponding coin quantity.
