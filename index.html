COIN SELLER POLICY

◆
Purpose
›

This policy outlines the eligibility requirements, operational standards, and performance expectations for agencies applying to become Official Coin Sellers on the RIZZ LIVE platform.

01
Eligibility Criteria
›
Minimum Requirement
📌 Qualification Threshold Agencies must generate 50,000,000 Coins (50M) monthly revenue to qualify as an Official Coin Seller.
Security Deposit Option
  • Agencies below the 50M target may apply with a $1,000 security deposit
  • Deposit is fully refunded once the monthly target is achieved
Maintenance Requirement
  • Approved sellers must sustain 50M monthly revenue
  • Failure for two consecutive months may result in seller status revocation
