4.1 Week 5 and Beyond
Fixed Target Structure
From Week 5 onwards, the Ruby target is fixed at 300,000 Rubies per week, maintaining the $15 weekly guarantee.

If an existing/old host passes the Prestige Policy audition, they are placed directly at Week 5 — no ramp-up period.

4.2 Requirements

• Breaking platform rules or laws
