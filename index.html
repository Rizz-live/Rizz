Identity & Trust

Host Authentication

How host identity is verified before going live and receiving payouts.

Note: This tab is a starter structure — plug in your exact KYC/verification requirements, vendor, and SLA before publishing.
1
Verification
🛡️
1. Why We Verify HostsTrust, safety & safe payouts
＋

Every host is verified before they can go live and withdraw earnings. This protects the community from fake accounts, keeps payouts secure, and confirms every host meets the platform's minimum age requirement.

📄
2. Documents RequiredWhat you'll need to submit
＋
  • Government-issued Photo ID — passport, national ID, or driver's licence
  • Live Selfie — matching the name and photo on the ID
  • Proof of Age 18+ — verified from the submitted ID
  • Payout Details — bank account or UPI matching the verified name
🧭
3. Verification StepsSubmit → Review → Approved
＋
  • Step 1 — Submit: Upload ID and selfie inside the app
  • Step 2 — Review: Our team checks your documents, typically within 24–48 hours
  • Step 3 — Decision: You're notified in-app once approved or if more info is needed
  • Step 4 — Go Live: Approved hosts can stream and request withdrawals immediately
2
Outcomes & Data
✅
4. Approval & RejectionWhat happens either way
＋
Approved

Full host access is unlocked — go live, join Rookie/Prestige policies, and withdraw earnings.

Rejected

Common reasons: blurry or mismatched ID, selfie doesn't match ID, under the minimum age, or expired document. You may resubmit corrected documents.

🔒
5. Data Privacy & SecurityHow your documents are handled
＋
  • Documents are used only for identity verification and payout matching
  • Stored encrypted and accessed only by the verification team
  • Never shared with viewers, agencies, or third parties
🔁
6. Re-verificationBank, name, or agency changes
＋
  • Changing payout bank/UPI details requires re-verification with matching ID
  • Legal name changes require an updated government ID
  • Agency transfers follow the Agency Change Rules in the Video/Audio policy tabs
Strict Rule

Submitting fake, edited, or someone else's documents results in a permanent ban.
