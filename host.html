1.3 Rules
  • Withdrawal Rate: 20,000 Ruby = 1 USD
  • Day Change: Day Changes Daily at 10:30 PM (IST)
  • Weekly Period: Tasks counted Sunday 10:30 PM to Sunday 10:30 PM (IST)
  • Valid Day: 1 day = Minimum 120 minutes (2 hours) Live
  • Valid Hour: 60+ minutes = 1 valid hour
  • Minimum Live Time: Any session below 30 minutes is not counted
  • Bonus Payment: Bonuses credited every Monday (in Rubies)
  • Withdrawal Rules: Allowed once per day, Minimum withdrawal = $10 USD
Agency Change Rules

A host can change agency if:

  • No live for 120 days
  • Cannot contact agency for 2 weeks
  • Both host and agency agree
⚠️ STRICT RULE Any misuse or cheating during transfer → Warning / Suspension / Ban

4.1 Requirements

Hosts must:

  • Look clean and presentable
  • Follow schedule and complete hours
  • Keep background neat
  • Maintain good audio & video
  • Actively engage with viewers
  • Follow team instructions and join activities
4.2 Removal from Sensual Host Policy

Host will be removed if:

  • Not streaming regularly or showing bad attitude
  • Ignoring or disrespecting viewers
  • Messy setup with no improvement
  • Not following team instructions
  • Poor stream quality without fixing
  • Breaking platform rules or laws
